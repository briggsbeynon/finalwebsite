Lacrosse is one of the fastest sports on foot, here's why.

Posted by Briggs Beynon on April 17, 2025

Lacrosse is often called the fastest sport on two feet because of its unmatched combination of speed, agility, and nonstop action. Players sprint up and down the field at full throttle, making quick passes and sharp cuts while trying to dodge defenders and maintain control of a small, fast-moving ball. The game requires lightning-fast reflexes, intense hand-eye coordination, and the ability to make split-second decisions, all while under constant pressure from opponents. With a 360-degree style of play and the constant transition between offense and defense, lacrosse keeps both players and spectators on their toes, earning its title as one of the most dynamic and fast-paced sports around.
